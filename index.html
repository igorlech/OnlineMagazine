ABOUT MAGAZINE

This magazine was created to introduce beginners to the world of drums. You can find there lots of valuable pieces of advice, as well as tutorials and basic knowledge. If you want to see your articles here, don’t hesitate to contact us.

WHY DRUMS?

It could be often heard that drumming looks cool. This might be indeed right, but there are many more reasons to play drums. It was proven that it has positive influence on human mind: it helps with coordination and concentration, as well as reduces stress. It could be even a great way of meditation or a hobby! So… what are you waiting for? Let’s start!
